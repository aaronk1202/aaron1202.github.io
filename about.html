About Red Balloon Construction

Built Around Quality, Safety and Service

Red Balloon Construction provides friendly, dependable multi-discipline support for commercial clients, combining practical site experience with a professional approach from first enquiry to final handover.

We work across commercial environments, universities, schools, colleges and facilities management sites, delivering construction, maintenance and passive fire protection services with a strong focus on workmanship, safety and clear communication.

As a one-stop contractor, we combine our own skilled team with trusted specialist support to manage projects clearly from start to finish. Our team is DBS approved for education environments and experienced in working safely around live operational buildings with minimal disruption.

We are CHAS and SafeContractor approved, hold CSCS Gold in construction, and are IPAF certified for external access and working at height. Our passive fire protection capability is supported by BM Trada Q-Mark fire door maintenance and installation certification, along with Hasman certification for fire damper servicing and Envirograf systems for passive fire protection works.
