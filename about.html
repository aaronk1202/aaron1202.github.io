About Red Balloon Construction

Built Around Quality, Safety and Service

Red Balloon Construction provides friendly, dependable multi-discipline support across residential and commercial projects, combining practical site experience with a professional approach from first enquiry to final handover.

Our services cover everything from large-scale construction sites to smaller maintenance jobs. As a one-stop company, Red Balloon brings together our own team with trusted specialist sub-contractors, allowing projects to be managed and controlled seamlessly from start to finish.

With all key working areas covered, we deliver a fully integrated service that supports efficiency, consistency and high-quality results. We approach every project with the same focus on workmanship, integrity and practical solutions.

We also have strong experience in education environments, including schools, colleges and academies, where safety, compliance and minimal disruption are essential. From routine inspections to essential repairs, we provide tailored support that keeps buildings safe, functional and well maintained.
