About Red Balloon Construction

Built Around Quality, Safety and Service

Red Balloon Construction is a one-stop, multi-discipline contractor supporting commercial clients, universities, schools and facilities management companies across construction, maintenance and passive fire protection.

We combine our own skilled team with trusted specialist support to manage projects clearly and efficiently from start to finish. Our work covers carpentry, building, maintenance, high-level access works and compliance-led passive fire protection.

We place particular focus on education environments, where safe working, DBS-approved teams, minimal disruption and clear communication are essential. Our team has experience supporting schools, colleges, universities and commercial estates with practical, responsive building services.

Our approach is straightforward: understand the site, plan the work properly, maintain professional standards and deliver a clean, safe finish.
