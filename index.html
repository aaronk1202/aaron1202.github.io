About Red Balloon Construction

Built Around Quality, Safety and Service

Red Balloon Construction provides friendly, dependable multi-discipline support for commercial clients, combining practical site experience with a professional approach from first enquiry to final handover.

We work across a variety of commercial environments, delivering construction services with a strong focus on workmanship, responsiveness and safe delivery.

From carpentry and general building works to specialist passive fire protection and fire stopping, we help clients complete projects to a high standard with clear communication and a flexible attitude.

Whether you need support on refurbishment works, planned maintenance or compliance-led fire stopping packages, Red Balloon Construction brings a straightforward and reliable service across Kent to London.

What clients can expect

  • Professional support for commercial projects of different sizes
  • Friendly service and a practical approach on site
  • Strong attention to safety, detail and finish quality
  • Flexible multi-trade capability from carpentry to fire stopping
  • Coverage across Kent, Greater London and surrounding areas
